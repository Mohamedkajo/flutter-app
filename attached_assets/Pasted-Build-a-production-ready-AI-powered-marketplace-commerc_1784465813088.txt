Build a production-ready AI-powered marketplace, commerce, logistics, and ERP platform called "Cargo". The platform should support Web, Android, iOS, and Tablets using Flutter for the frontend and Odoo 18 Community as the backend ERP with PostgreSQL.

The application must have a premium modern UI exactly following the attached design reference with a clean white background, elegant purple primary color, rounded cards, smooth animations, large product images, premium typography, glassmorphism, and Apple-quality user experience. The design must remain consistent across all screens and be fully responsive.

Cargo is a multi-vendor marketplace where every business has its own public storefront and profile. Businesses can be restaurants, cafes, bakeries, pharmacies, grocery stores, supermarkets, fashion stores, electronics shops, flower shops, furniture stores, beauty salons, service providers, warehouses, or any online business.
